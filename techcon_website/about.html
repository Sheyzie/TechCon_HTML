Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local meetup of tech enthusiasts and has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of participants each year. Over the years, we've hosted in multiple cities, from San Francisco to Nairobi, promoting innovation and collaboration across the tech world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the next generation of innovators, foster inclusive technology communities, and create spaces where groundbreaking ideas are shared and celebrated.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Smith

Dr. Ada Smith is a pioneer in artificial intelligence and ethics. She delivered a keynote on responsible AI in 2021 that was hailed as one of the most influential talks in TechCon history.

Lee Zhang

Founder of OpenDev, Lee Zhang shared insights on open-source communities and their role in modern software development during TechCon 2022.

Maria Fernandez

Maria Fernandez spoke at TechCon 2023 about the future of cybersecurity and how to protect user data in a connected world.
